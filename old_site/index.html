About the Competition Design & Performance Challenge

The International Quarter Scale Competition is a student design event where university teams conceive, build, and validate a quarter-scale tractor. Teams are judged on engineering design, innovation, manufacturability, safety, and performance in a series of dynamic events.

What you’ll find on this site:
  • Official overall & event-by-event results
  • Real-time scoring once events begin
  • Final rankings and downloadable score sheets
