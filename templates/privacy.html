This website is operated for the International Quarter Scale Competition (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use this site.

Information we collect

Analytics and usage data

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors use this site, including which pages are visited, how long visitors stay, and what devices and browsers are used. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • Pages and content you view
  • Your approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Device and browser information
  • Referring website or link
  • Session duration and general interaction with the site

Google Analytics “Google Signals” and logged-in Google accounts

We have enabled certain Google Analytics features that allow Google to collect additional data from users who are signed in to their Google accounts and have turned on Ads Personalization. This is sometimes referred to as Google Signals.

When these features are active, Google may associate your visit to this site with your Google account and combine this information with data from other sites that use Google services. Google then provides us with aggregated and anonymized reports, such as:

  • More detailed demographics and interests reports
  • Cross-device reporting (for example, when users visit from both mobile and desktop)
  • Better understanding of how visitors return to and move through the site over time

We only see this information in aggregated form and never receive your individual Google account information or identity from Google.

Cookies and similar technologies

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and store analytics data. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. If you disable cookies, some features of this site may not work as intended, but basic browsing should still be possible.

How we use this information

We use the analytics information described above to:

  • Understand how visitors find and use the site
  • Monitor site performance and fix issues
  • Improve layout, navigation, and content
  • Plan and prioritize features related to events, teams, tractors, and results

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not attempt to identify individual visitors from analytics data.

Google’s use of data

Google’s use of information collected through Google Analytics is governed by Google’s own policies. For more information, please see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services

How to opt out of Google Analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics in several ways:

  • Adjust your Google Ads Personalization settings in your Google account: https://adssettings.google.com
  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Use your browser’s settings to block or delete cookies.

Other data you may provide

If you contact us directly (for example, by email or through a form), we may receive information such as your name, email address, and the contents of your message. We use this information only to respond to your inquiry and manage our communication with you.

Data retention

Analytics data is retained in Google Analytics according to the retention settings configured in our Google Analytics account. These settings may change over time as we review our needs and obligations.

Children’s privacy

This site is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the site after changes have been posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
